Power of Habits

We often overestimate the importance of that one defining moments that might occur in our life and underestimate the value of making small improvement on a daily basis. We often think that to get success we need to do massive action and put so much pressure on ourselves to improve in such a drastic manner. Meaning improving just by 1% isn't even noticeable but in long run in a more meaning to get the desired results.

A small change in our habits is similar to the effect of shifting the route of an airplane by just a few degree.

Therefore some important things to note :

  • All big things come from small beginnings.
  • Forget the goals and Focus on the system instead. Fix the inputs and the output fix themselves.
  • We set goals in order to achieve them and win the game. But the purpose of building a system or habit is to continue playing the game. If we think in a long term perspective, it is not about a single accomplishment but a process of continuous improvement and ultimately, it is our commitment to the process that will determine our progress.
